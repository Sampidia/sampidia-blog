About SamPidia Community

The SamPidia community started 2018 on WhatsApp group as an outreach platform to guide applicants seeking admission into higher institutions. However, the community as grown beyond the use of only WhatsApp, in other words, to reach out to vast applicants and graduates across Nigeria the need to get a website arose and therefore sampidia secure the ownership of the domain name www.sampidia.com.
